Meet Lucky!

The fiesty ball of yappery

Lucky is an energetic pup with a feisty attitude. Despite that, he can also be loving and a very rewarding pet to have. He can be a tough pup to handle from time to time, but he just needs a bit of love and care!

Lucky is a 3 pound chihuahua. He is only one year old, so he's a very curious creature who tends to explore quite a bit. But be sure to watch him! He's very small and easy to lose! He does require a lot of attention and can pretty barky if he doesn't get it. But he be easily entertained with a chew toy and a rope.

Lucky was found by one of our volunteers eating from a garbage can outside of a Panera. He was skinny, mal-nourished, and shivering in the cold. He was taken into Ashworth's and given a proper meal and warm blankets. Only thing he needs now is a home!

Do you believe you may be a fitting owner? Please consider adopting Lucky and welcoming him to a new home!
